# GDV Evaluation Tasks

## Goal

This short evaluation checks whether the static GDV visualizations communicate the intended insights clearly.

## Participants

Use 3 participants if possible. They can be classmates, friends or family members with basic football knowledge.

## Procedure

Show the final GDV figures in this order:

1. Build-up types
2. Passes vs build-up duration
3. Direct vs patient teams
4. Finishing efficiency vs tournament progress
5. Build-up type share by team
6. Spain case study

Ask the participant to think aloud while answering the tasks.

## Tasks

### Task 1

Look at the build-up type chart. Do goals at the FIFA World Cup 2022 seem to happen more often after quick, medium or long build-ups?

### Task 2

Look at the passes vs duration scatterplot. Does a higher number of passes usually mean a longer attack duration?

### Task 3

Look at the direct vs patient teams chart. Name one team that appears to score more directly and one team that appears to score after longer build-ups.

### Task 4

Look at the finishing efficiency vs tournament progress chart. Does the chart suggest that efficient teams always went further in the tournament?

### Task 5

Look at the Spain case study. Can you understand the pass sequence before the goal?

## Feedback questions

1. Which figure was easiest to understand?
2. Which figure was most confusing?
3. Were the labels and legends clear?
4. Did the visualizations answer the research question?
5. What would you improve?
